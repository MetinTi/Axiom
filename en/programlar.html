Salesperson's AI Curriculum (3 Core Stages)

Stage 1

Hyper-Intelligence & Prep

Before client meetings, we use AI agents to scan company financial statements, industry trends, and the prospect's digital footprint to map micro-needs. You go to the meeting not to sell a product, but to solve the exact problem discussed in their boardroom that morning.

Stage 2

AI-Powered Micro-Persuasion

Before entering the field, salespeople train in our custom Axiom AI simulator that mimics the prospect's psychological profile. They experience the toughest objection scenarios live. The field is no longer a place for trial and error.

Stage 3

Co-Pilot for Closing & Follow-up

We streamline drafting proposals, extracting actions from meeting notes, and writing personalized follow-up emails using AI co-pilots in seconds. Salespeople spend 70% less time on admin work, shifting all focus to human-to-human persuasion.
