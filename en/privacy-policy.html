Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Axiom, we attach great importance to your privacy and the protection of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you visit our site or use our contact forms, how we use it, and how we ensure the security of your data.

1. Personal Data Collected

We only collect data shared directly by you in the following cases:

  • **Contact Form:** Your name, surname, company name, email address, the type of program you are interested in, and the message content you submit.
  • **Analytical Data:** Anonymous usage information such as IP address, browser type, duration of visit, and click stream data collected via cookies to improve the performance of our site.

2. Purposes of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form and offer custom training proposals,
  • To improve our platform and provide a more qualitative user experience,
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard encryption and technical security measures to protect your data. Your personal data is stored in our secure servers for the period necessary to provide services to you or within the scope of legal retention periods. It is never shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to optimize the user experience and analyze site traffic. You can completely block or restrict cookies from your browser settings; however, in this case, some features of our site may not function fully.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection regulations (such as GDPR or KVKK), you have the right to learn whether your personal data is processed, request information, demand correction if it is incomplete or incorrect, and request the deletion of your personal data.

6. Contact

For any questions or requests regarding our Privacy Policy or your rights over your data, you can reach us at **info@axiom.com.tr**.
